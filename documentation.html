Cosmofeed — Zoom Integration Walkthru

Hi! I'm here to help walk you thru integrating your Zoom account with Cosmofeed. Cosmofeed let's you create a beautiful website for your event to share on social media and collect RSVPs. Our Zoom integration allows you to login with Zoom to automatically create Zoom meetings associated with your events.

Installation

  1. Visit Cosmofeed Zoom Integration page
  2. Click "Login With Zoom"
  3. Select "Authorize" on the Zoom page
  4. That should send you back to Cosmofeed where you can now create an event

Troubleshooting

If you have any trouble installing the app, we have live chat on our website. Just click the green "Need Help?" button on the bottom right of our website to chat with us.

Uninstalling the App and Deauthorizing the App

  1. Visit the Zoom marketplace: https://marketplace.zoom.us/
  2. Click on the Cosmofeed application
  3. Go to the "Manage" tab
  4. Click "Uninstall"

Usage — Generate Zoom Meetings for Your Events

Luma let's you create beautiful event websites. Our Zoom integration will automatically create a Zoom meeting associated with your event.

  1. Sign into Cosmofeed at Cosmofeed Zoom Integration page
  2. Click "Create Event"
  3. Fill out the form to create your event
  4. You will now have an event with an attached Zoom meeting
